Introduction

Hello, world! Welcome to my personal homepage. My name is Minjie Fan（范敏杰）. I am currently a third-year Ph.D. student in the Department of Statistics at University of California, Davis. I received my Bachelor of Science degree in Mathematics from Fudan University. During that period, I worked with Prof. Shushang Zhu for robust portfolio management. In the first summer as a Ph.D. student, I worked with Prof. Thomas Lee for image segmentation. Since the second year, I have started working on topics in spatial statistics with Prof. Thomas Lee, Prof. Debashis Paul and Dr. Tomoko Matsuo. The topics involve modeling small-scale electric potential fields via spherical Needlets, constructing cross-covariance functions for tangent vector fields on the sphere and robust cokriging based on a minimax criterion.
